This page displays the investments made by the Cameroon national government in 2011. The figures are budgeted expenditure, not actual amounts. Investments are managed by the Planning Ministry (MINEPAT).

The data has been re-organized into a system of focus sectors which group some of the chapters present in the original budget document into larger categories. The sector system is loosely based on the UN's COFOG scheme.
