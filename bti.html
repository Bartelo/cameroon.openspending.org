Budget Transparency Index

The survey measures fifteen criteria regarding the managament of local council budgets: are the documents produced, published and disseminated in a way that allows media and citizens to review the budget?

The data was collected in 2010 and 2011 and includes all but two of the municipalities of the Adamawa region.
